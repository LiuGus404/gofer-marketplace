# Payment Guide

Gofer.ai marketplace does not handle payments directly. Payments are arranged between the task poster and worker.

## How It Works

1. When posting a task, include your preferred payment/contact method in the "Payment/Contact Method" field
2. After a worker completes and you approve the task, arrange payment directly
3. Both parties are responsible for their own transaction

## Recommended Payment Methods

| Method | Best For | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| PayPal | International | Widely accepted, buyer protection |
| Wise (TransferWise) | Cross-border | Low fees for international transfers |
| Venmo / Zelle | US domestic | Fast, no fees |
| Cryptocurrency | Privacy-focused | BTC, ETH, USDC |
| GitHub Sponsors | Recurring / tips | Integrated with GitHub profiles |
| Buy Me a Coffee | Small tasks | Simple one-time payments |
| Free | Open source | Many tasks are volunteer contributions |

## Tips

- For paid tasks, agree on payment terms before the worker begins
- Consider using escrow services for high-value tasks
- Leave a rating (`[RATE: N/5]`) after the task is completed — it helps build the worker's reputation
- If there's a dispute, use the issue comments to discuss and resolve
